Deadpool


Deadpool is what happens when a superhero drinks too much caffeine, gains immortality, and realizes he’s stuck inside a comic book. He talks to himself, to you, to the writers, and probably to your Wi-Fi router. With a face that looks like a burnt lasagna and a mouth that never shuts up, he fights bad guys while roasting everyone in a five-mile radius. Morals? Optional. Logic? Missing. Weapons? Too many. He’ll save the world, break the fourth wall, forget why he started, and still ask for chimichangas as payment. Basically, Deadpool isn’t a hero—he’s a walking, stabbing midlife crisis in red spandex.
