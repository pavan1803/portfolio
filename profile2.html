Loki


Loki is the God of Mischief reborn into a new destiny beyond villainy — a clever, unpredictable trickster pulled from his old timeline and forced to face his own choices at the Time Variance Authority (TVA). Though once selfish and scheming, in the series he slowly learns empathy, purpose, and courage as he hunts variants, questions fate, and fights to protect the fragile multiverse. Loki’s journey is one of self-discovery and reluctant heroism: not perfect, still mischievous, but increasingly willing to do what’s right, even at great personal cost.
